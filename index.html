After booking, we may put a hold on your card for the cleaning amount but nothing is charged until the cleaning is completed.

1 MAID FOR 1 HOUR

Purchasing 1 hour of maid services gets you 1 insured, bonded, and background checked maid, with supplies, for 1 hour of house cleaning. If we send multiple maids to your home, then each maid's time is billed at the hourly rate then added together.For example, a 12-hour cleaning is too much for 1 maid, so we may send 2 or 3 maids to your home. 2 maids would complete a 12-hour cleaning in 6 clock hours, 3 maids would do it in 4 clock hours. Either way, you get 12 total hours of cleaning.
